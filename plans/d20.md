# Implement D20 Dice Scheme

The goal is to introduce a new "D20" dice scheme that uses two 20-sided dice to determine baseball outcomes. The first roll resolves whether a batter gets a hit or is out, and the second roll resolves the specific type of hit or out.

## Proposed Probability Tables

**Roll 1 (Resolution Roll)**
| Roll Result | Category |
| :---: | :---: |
| 1 - 15 | Out (75%) |
| 16 - 20 | Hit / Safe (25%) |

**Roll 2 (Out Type - if Roll 1 is 1-15)**
| Roll Result | Outcome Type | Probability |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| 1 - 5 | Strikeout | 25% |
| 6 - 10 | Ground Out | 25% |
| 11 - 15 | Fly Out | 25% |
| 16 - 18 | Foul Out | 15% |
| 19 - 20 | Double Play ✨ | 10% |

> [!NOTE]
> ✨ Double plays will revert to a Ground Out if there are no runners on base, or if there are already 2 outs.

**Roll 2 (Hit Type - if Roll 1 is 16-20)**
| Roll Result | Outcome Type | Probability |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| 1 - 5 | Walk | 25% |
| 6 - 13 | Single | 40% |
| 14 - 17 | Double | 20% |
| 18 | Triple | 5% |
| 19 - 20 | Home Run | 10% |
